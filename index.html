About

Harry Karambizi is a Web Development Immersive Student at General Assembly in Atlanta, GA. He is a social entrepreur who believes in progressive, positive change through purposeful business strategy. With a background in Marketing, Sales and Hospitality, his passion is to develop software and web apps that can put power and upward mobility in the hands of all who desire it.

The Web Development Immersive at General Assembly is an intense program that teaches students the fundamentals of programming and product development, full stack web development to create valuable development team members and life-long learners.
